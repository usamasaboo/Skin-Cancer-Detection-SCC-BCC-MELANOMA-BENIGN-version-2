ARCHITECTURE SUMMARY

The core model utilizes transfer learning on a pre-trained EfficientNet-B0 backbone fine-tuned explicitly for dermatological macro-photography and dermoscopic imagery.

CLINICAL HYBRID ENGINE

Outputs are strictly routed through an OpenCV heuristic layer assessing structural asymmetry, border compactness, color variance (HSV), and texture density to combat false positives in extreme edge cases (glare, body hair, plain dark skin).
